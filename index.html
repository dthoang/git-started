Aquaman

Aquaman is an American comic book super hero published under DC comics. Having telepathic communicative powers, invulnerability, super-strength, and a good moral compass as tools for his disposal, he is a friend of fish, an ally to Atlantians, and a helper to humankind all at once.

Aquaman is the head-liner of his own self-titled series, but he is also an important character in the Justice League and Super Friends series, among many other appearances in the DC Comics Universe.
